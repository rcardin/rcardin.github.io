I had the pleasure of giving a talk at the Scala Days 2025 in Lausanne. The talk was titled "Yo Dawg, Heard You Want To FlatMap Your Direct-Style". I talked about the challenges of using direct-style programming in Scala and how we can leverage the power of effect systems to make our code more expressive and easier to reason about. The talk was well-received, and I had a great time interacting with the audience and answering their questions.
